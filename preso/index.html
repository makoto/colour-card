Colour Cards

Makoto Inoue, Mark Durrant & Nick Johnson

The Brief

Design a solution for storing Keys and Passphrases, capable of offering the opportunity to reconnect the owner with the Wallet and contents should the link be severed.

The Problem

Why not just generate keys yourself?

  • Few people make regular backups
  • If your computer dies, so does your bank account
  • If your computer is hacked, so is your bank account

The Problem

Why not trust a third party?

  • Centralised services are hacked with depressing regularity
  • You're trusting them with everything
  • If they go offline, so do you

Colour Cards

  • Combine the best attributes of both solutions
  • Enter a password to generate a card with an account
  • Neither card nor password alone can access your account
